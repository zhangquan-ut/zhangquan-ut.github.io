My GitHub

I am a Ph.D. student in Department of Information, Risk and Operation Management, McCombs School of Business, University of Texas at Austin. My research interest includes statistics, Bayesian methods and machine learning. I am focused on developing probability models in machine learning, developing Gibbs sampler for complex models via data augmentation, and speeding up algorithms using variational approaches. I am also experienced in Bayesian design and analysis of clinical trials. I am now working on non-linear classifiers with probablistic and geometric interpretations that can be applied to marketing and e-commerce research.

I am a comedian and perform stand-up comedy which is called crosstalk, or Xiangsheng. If only one thing can be written in my CV, it must be that I performed Xiangsheng for 3,000 audiences at Peking University 2012 graduation party.
